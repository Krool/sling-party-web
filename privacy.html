Sling Party - Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Sling Party ("the Game") is a free game developed by Krool ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the Game collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The Game uses Firebase (by Google) for optional online features. When you use these features, the following data may be collected:

  • Anonymous identifier:Firebase Authentication creates a random anonymous ID to identify your device. No email, name, or login is required.
  • Leaderboard scores:If you submit a score to the leaderboard, your player name (chosen by you) and score are stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Chat messages:If you send a message in the in-game chat, your player name and message text are stored in Firebase Firestore.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or any personal information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad tracking.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties (beyond Firebase infrastructure).

Local Storage

The Game saves your progress, settings, and preferences locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google) processes the anonymous identifier and online data described above. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we only use anonymous identifiers and optional player-chosen display names, no personal information is collected from any user.

Data Deletion

To delete your data, clear the app's local storage (uninstall and reinstall) or contact us to request deletion of any leaderboard or chat data associated with your anonymous ID.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will be updated accordingly.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at: github.com/Krool/sling-party-web/issues
