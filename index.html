What is Sling Party?

Physics Destruction

Sling heroes into block fortresses and watch them crumble with satisfying physics.

Roguelite Runs

3-floor runs across unique biomes. Every run is different with procedural maps and random relics.

8 Hero Classes

Warriors, Mages, Rogues, Druids and more — each with unique passives and skill trees.

30+ Relics

Collect powerful relics that transform your strategy. Watch out for curses.

Camp Upgrades

Build up your camp between runs to unlock new heroes, upgrades, and perks.

3 Biomes

Battle through Goblin Wastes, Frozen Peaks, and the Infernal Keep.
