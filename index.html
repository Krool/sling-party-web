About the Game

Sling Party is a physics-based roguelike where you launch heroes from a giant slingshot into enemy fortresses. Drag back, aim, release. Your hero arcs through the air, smashes through blocks, and fights whatever's left standing.

Each run takes you through three biomes with procedurally generated maps. Grab relics, level up skill trees, and deal with random events along the way. Between runs, upgrade your camp to unlock new heroes and permanent bonuses.

No ads. No in-app purchases. No paywalls.
